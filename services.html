Our Services

Hot Stone Massage

Hot stone massage incorporates heated stones placed on the body and used by the therapist to relax muscles and promote circulation.

Warmth of the stones enhances muscle relaxation and can be deeply soothing.

Sauna Aromatherapy Kit

A sauna aromatherapy kit includes essential oils and blends designed to be used in saunas to enhance relaxation and mood.

Clients can add a few drops of these oils to the sauna rocks or a designated diffuser for a customized and aromatic experience.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage uses firm pressure and slow strokes to target deeper layers of muscle tissue, addressing chronic pain and tension.

This type of massage is effective for athletes and individuals with specific areas of tension.

Aromatherapy Massage

An aromatherapy massage combines massage techniques with the use of essential oils to address various concerns, from stress to pain.

The choice of essential oils can customize the massage experience, addressing specific needs.

Himalayan Salt Sauna Session

A Himalayan salt sauna incorporates Himalayan salt walls or blocks to enhance the air quality and provide mineral-rich benefits.

This unique sauna experience can help improve respiratory health and create a tranquil environment.

Hydrating Body Wrap

A hydrating body wrap utilizes moisturizing products, often rich in shea butter or aloe vera, to nourish and hydrate the skin deeply.

This treatment is perfect for dry skin, as it helps restore moisture and leaves the skin feeling soft and supple.

Gentle Exfoliating Facial Peel

A gentle exfoliating facial peel uses mild acids to remove dead skin cells, revealing a smoother and brighter complexion.

This treatment can improve the appearance of fine lines, acne scars, and uneven skin tone.

Traditional Dry Sauna Session

A traditional dry sauna uses heated rocks or elements to create high-temperature, dry heat within a wooden enclosure.

Clients enjoy a dry heat experience, promoting sweating and relaxation, along with potential health benefits.
