about us

We are Inner Peace Cosmetology and Spa Center,

established in 2021 as a sanctuary of beauty, relaxation, and transformation. From the moment you step inside, our serene environment and expert staff create a space for tailored experiences, including facials, massages, hair, and nail services.

Beyond being a spa, we're a place of self-discovery and empowerment, offering holistic wellness programs and workshops. Whether you seek personal transformation or a moment of solace, we're dedicated to helping you rediscover your inner peace and make beauty a spiritual experience.
